Lemon Sugar Cookies


Easy lemon cookies rolled in white sugar.

Makes ~24 cookies!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup (230g) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 & 1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp lemon zest (you may also substitute with an equal amount of lemon juice)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar (for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Beat together the butter, sugar, and lemon zest for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Add in the lemon juice and egg, and beat them together to combine them.
  3. Sift together baking soda, flour, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing by hand with a spatula until combined.
  4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30-60 minutes.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350F, and measure out granulated sugar for rolling the cookies.
  6. Remove the dough from the fridge. Use a cookie scoop to form balls about 1 inch in size or smaller if desired, rolling each ball in the sugar.
  7. Place cookies 2 inches apart on a baking sheet and bake for 11-13 minutes or until golden at the edges.
  8. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
