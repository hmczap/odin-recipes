Easiest Red Velvet Cookies


Red velvet cookies - only three ingredients required! Makes ~24 cookies.

Tip: you can add white chocolate chips into the dough for an extra touch.

Ingredients:

  • 1 box red velvet cake mix (any kind will do)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil (you can also substitute for any other neutral oil, including olive oil)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375F.
  2. Mix ingredients well in a bowl. You may add 1/2 tsp of water or oil if the mixture is too dry - this depends on the type of cake mix you have on hand.
  3. Measure and roll cookies into 1 inch balls. Place dough 2 inches apart on lined cookie sheet
  4. Bake for 7 minutes or until the edges start to brown.
  5. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for 1-5 minutes before transferring to a cooling rack.
